About Us

Airbnb is a global online marketplace founded in 2007 by Brian Chesky, Joe Gebbia, and Nathan Blecharczyk. It allows hosts to offer unique stays, experiences, and services to guests, fostering authentic connections with local communities. The company has grown from a simple "Air Bed and Breakfast" concept to a platform with millions of hosts and guest arrivals worldwide, emphasizing community, experiences, and travel
