Coastal Escape — Great Ocean Road

Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Great Ocean Road, a spectacular coastal drive known for its dramatic cliffs, sweeping ocean views, and charming seaside towns. This 1-day tour starting from Lorne offers an unforgettable adventure, perfect for nature lovers and photographers alike.

What’s Included:

  • Guided scenic drive along the coast
  • Stops at iconic landmarks like the Twelve Apostles and Loch Ard Gorge
  • Lunch at a seaside cafe in Apollo Bay
  • Opportunities for hiking and beach walks

Tour Details:

Price: $250 / 1 Day Tour

Location: Lorne, Victoria

Availability: Daily tours, booking recommended
